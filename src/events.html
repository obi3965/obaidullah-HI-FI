Linn Klinik Event

Free turntable service - Get 15% off, plus the opportunity to own a fluted plinth

It's 45 years into the LP12 revolution so to celebrate this and the launch of of Linn’s latest LP12 upgrades, you can get a tempting 15% off an additional LP12 upgrade when you purchase either Urika II or new Lingo 4 power supply. Plus, we’re reintroducing the Sondek LP12’s original fluted plinth design. Available for a limited time only and exclusively for those buying Urika II or Lingo. As well as servicing your LP12 on the day we will also be doing back to back demonstrations of the Urika II and new Lingo 4 power supply with our very own Tony Franchi (Linn’s technical trainer now part of our specialist team at Hi-Fi Corner). No registration required, just turn up on the day.

When: 8th March 2018

Where: Hi-Fi Corner Edinburgh, 1 Haddington Place, Edinburgh, EH7 4AE
